About Us

Your Neighbourhood Repair Specialist on Cranbrook Road

Located at 563 Cranbrook Road in the heart of Gants Hill, Quickie Heel Bar has been the go-to destination for shoe repairs, key cutting, and clothing alterations for the local community in Ilford and beyond.

We offer a wide range of repair services — from replacing worn heels and damaged soles to cutting keys with precision and mending broken zips. Our craftsmen take real pride in restoring your belongings to their very best condition.

Open early from 7:45 AM, Monday to Saturday — so you can drop off before work, collect at lunch, or pop in on the way home. We're cash only, but there are ATMs and banks conveniently close by on Cranbrook Road.
